Foundations in Performance Excellence for Government

This class provides an overview of the power of continuous improvement and lean thinking, and equips you with a solid base of knowledge and techniques you can apply immediately to improve performance in your own government organization.

In this five-module series, you will:
  • Discuss why lean thinking and continuous improvement is important to long-term success.
  • Discover how forward-thinking public sector organizations are using these techniques to create a better environment for their people and deliver more value to their residents faster and at a lower cost by improving how they do their work.
  • Discuss specific tools and methodologies to eliminate idle time, bureaucracy, and unnecessary redundancy.
  • Explain the benefits of visualizing work processes and systems, creating a safe and organized work environment, and developing standard work that provides a foundation for continuous improvement.

Included modules:

Introduction to Lean Government

Learn the fundamental principles of Lean Government and understand how Lean Government thinking, tools and techniques can be deployed in your organization to drive excellence by eliminating waste and reducing costs, while delivering more value to customers.

Eight Wastes in Government Processes

Learn how to identify and analyze eight categories of wastes in government processes. Learn how removing process waste contributes to sustainability by delivering more value to customers in less time while consuming the fewest resources possible.

5S for Government

Learn how to use 5S methodology to create and maintain an organized, clean and safe work environment in which government organizations can deliver and achieve high performance.

Mapping the Process for Government

Learn how the use of process mapping tools can help you make work visible with the objectives of eliminating waste, improving workflow and identifying opportunities to improve performance.

Standard Work for Government

Learn why Standard Work is necessary to provide a basis for continuous improvement, and how to develop Standard Work for government operations.

Introduction to Lean for Government

Included in the Foundations in Performance Excellence for Government class, this module provides an introduction into the power of the Lean methodology to transform government organizations in a manner that provides improved efficiency and effectiveness while galvanizing and empowering government employees.

Learning Objectives:
  • Define Lean Government and discuss its importance, benefits, and objectives.
  • Describe the fundamental principles of Lean Government.
  • Explain the lean approach to performance improvement.
  • Identify specific Lean Government tools, methodology, and levels of deployment.

How do you know if an improvement really is an improvement?

Learning about and applying Lean Metrics will give you the facts you need to be confident you are on the path to excellence. Introduction to Lean for Government will provide the history of lean methodology, examples of how lean is being applied in the public sector, and an overview of its basic components.

Performance Excellence in Action

This five module cross-industry curriculum translates the concepts learned in the Foundations in Performance Excellence for Government class into action by providing practical, actionable solutions.

Do you have the desire to make your work easier and more effective and need practical solutions to begin achieving meaningful progress? The real learning on the journey of continuous improvement begins with application.

Included modules:

Developing Lean Thinkers

Learn what “lean thinking” is all about and why it’s critical in building a culture of performance excellence.

Going to Gemba

Going to Gemba is a simple, powerful concept that will help you see reality, show respect for workers, and provide important insights for identifying improvement opportunities. To “go to gemba” is to discover the truth.

A3 Problem Solving

This module builds on the lessons of direct observation and provides the student with a proven framework for problem-solving and communication – the A3 Report.

Kaizen Event

Kaizen Events, often called Rapid Improvement Events, are a great way to develop the problem-solving capabilities of your people and deliver positive results quickly that inspire and reward all involved with progress toward a high performance culture.

Lean Metrics

How do you know if an improvement really is an improvement? Learning about and applying Lean Metrics will give you the facts you need to be confident you are on the path to excellence.
